New home for spd.gr

October 31, 2019

I have started moving spd.gr to github pages. In other words…

  • From: Paid-for web hosting with an insultingly low and hard bandwidth limit
  • To: Free hosting, with a much higher as well as soft bandwidth limit

No PHP support, which is a good thing cause that means I have to get my act together and re-develop my content without it, which is something I’ve wanted to do for years…

Plus, I get to experiment with technologies that are totally new to me (e.g. Jekyll).
